Agentic AI Summary:

• Agentic AI refers to autonomous artificial intelligence systems that can perceive
  their environment, reason, plan, and make independent decisions to achieve specific
  goals with minimal human supervision — essentially mimicking human decision-making
  processes across complex, multi-step tasks.

• These AI agents are capable of coordinated action across digital systems, can adapt
  their behaviour based on environmental changes, and execute tasks in real-time while
  learning from their interactions — using tools like web search, file I/O, and APIs
  to act in the world rather than just respond to prompts.

• Agentic AI represents a significant advancement beyond traditional chatbots by
  enabling systems to operate autonomously across domains including research, coding,
  and business automation — driven by a repeating loop of: perceive → reason → act →
  observe, until the task is complete.
